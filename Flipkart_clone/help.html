Frequently Asked Questions

You can track your order by going to 'My Orders' section in your account. You'll find real-time tracking information and expected delivery dates there.

Most items can be returned within 7-30 days of delivery. The return period varies by product category. Items should be in original condition with tags and packaging.

Standard delivery takes 2-7 business days depending on your location. Express delivery is available in select cities with next-day delivery.

You can cancel your order before it's shipped by going to 'My Orders' and clicking on 'Cancel Order'. If the order has already shipped, you can return it after delivery.

We accept credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, wallets, EMI, and cash on delivery (where available). All payments are secured with bank-level encryption.
